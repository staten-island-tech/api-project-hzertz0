Rules

The Rules:

As you draw a card, a counter will go up by each card value, if the card drawn is equal to the value, or a Jack, then you must slap within 1 second.

If you fail to slap in time or attempt to draw a card, then the currently drawn cards are put back into the deck.

If you slap in time, then you get to hold onto the current pile drawn, the goal is to hold onto all 52 cards!
